# Actors and Roles

## Purpose

This document identifies all actors that interact with the Employee Lifecycle and Workforce Management Portal.

Actors represent users, administrators, managers, or systems that perform actions within the platform.

---

## Primary Actors

### 1. Employee

Represents a person employed by the organization.

Responsibilities:

* View profile
* Update personal information
* Apply for leave
* Access company policies
* View announcements
* Participate in onboarding activities

---

### 2. HR Administrator

Responsible for workforce administration and employee lifecycle management.

Responsibilities:

* Manage employee records
* Manage onboarding
* Manage offboarding
* Maintain organizational data
* Generate HR reports

---

### 3. Recruiter

Responsible for talent acquisition activities.

Responsibilities:

* Manage candidates
* Track hiring processes
* Coordinate interviews
* Create hiring records

---

### 4. Manager

Responsible for supervising employees and teams.

Responsibilities:

* Approve requests
* Review employee information
* Monitor team activities
* Track workforce performance

---

### 5. System Administrator

Responsible for platform administration.

Responsibilities:

* Manage users
* Manage roles
* Configure permissions
* Monitor system health
* Manage security settings

---

## Secondary Actors

### Executive Leadership

Consumes reports, analytics, and workforce insights.

### External Candidate

Potential employee participating in recruitment processes.

### AI Knowledge Assistant

Provides policy and knowledge retrieval using the RAG module.
